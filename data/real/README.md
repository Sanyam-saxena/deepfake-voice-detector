# Real Voice Samples

Place your **real human voice** audio files in this directory.

## Supported Formats
- WAV (`.wav`)
- MP3 (`.mp3`)
- FLAC (`.flac`)
- OGG (`.ogg`)
- M4A (`.m4a`)

## Examples
```
data/real/
├── human_speech_001.wav
├── human_speech_002.wav
├── interview_clip_01.mp3
└── podcast_sample.wav
```

## Tips
- Use high-quality recordings for better results
- Ensure audio is clear and contains actual speech
- Any duration is acceptable (will be processed to 3 seconds)
- Aim for at least 100+ samples for training

## Where to Find Real Voice Samples
- Record your own voice
- Free speech datasets (LibriSpeech, Common Voice)
- Public domain audio recordings
- Podcast clips (with proper permissions)
